Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your Tanzanian adventure.

What is the best time to visit Tanzania?
The best time for wildlife viewing is during the dry season, from June to October. For the Great Migration in the Serengeti, the best months are usually June and July. If you want to see the calving season, visit in January or February.
Do I need a visa to enter Tanzania?
Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Tanzania. You can apply for an e-visa online before your arrival or obtain a visa on arrival at major entry points. Please check the official Tanzanian immigration website for the latest requirements for your country.
Is Tanzania safe for tourists?
Tanzania is generally considered a safe country for tourists. However, like any travel destination, it's important to take common-sense precautions. Our professional guides are with you throughout your journey to ensure your safety and comfort.
What should I pack for a safari?
We recommend light, neutral-colored clothing, a warm jacket for early morning drives, comfortable walking shoes, a hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, and binoculars. Don't forget your camera!
Are vaccinations required?
Yellow Fever vaccination is mandatory if you are traveling from a country with risk of Yellow Fever transmission. We also recommend consulting your doctor about malaria prophylaxis and other recommended vaccinations like Hepatitis A and Typhoid.
